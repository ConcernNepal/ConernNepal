Expert Network

Concern Nepal has access to a highly skilled and diverse network, with expertise in areas such as governance, education, and development, making it well-equipped to address the complexities of community engagement.

Insights from Government Collaboration

Working with government bodies has highlighted gaps in knowledge and awareness on political matters. Many institutions lack impartial information on significant issues, which motivates Concern Nepal to empower youth with the skills and knowledge to understand and engage with political systems.

Raising Political Awareness

Concern Nepal sees it as crucial for youth to understand governmental functions, including bureaucracy and the roles of elected officials, so they can contribute meaningfully to governance and policy when they assume future leadership roles.

Influential Networ:

Concern Nepal’s broad network—including top politician, high level bureaucrats, government officials, opinion leaders, and stakeholders—positions it to offer comprehensive insights and facilitate meaningful training on political processes.
